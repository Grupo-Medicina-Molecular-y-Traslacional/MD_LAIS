Over the years, it has been generally accepted that the definition of global (or local) indices from LOVIs (Local Vertex Invariants),called here LAIs (Local Amino Acid Invariants), implies the summation of the contributions of the elements that constitute a given molecule (or graph). In this program, we introduce a series of aggregation operators that generalize the traditional method of obtaining global (or local) invariants by summation of the LAIs. 1) Norms (or Metrics),
